About Me.

I'm a 16-year-old Blockchain and Machine Learning enthusiast and developer. I spend my time learning about technology, coding and building things to solve important problems in healthcare, finance and energy.

I've worked as an ML developer with top companies like TD Bank and Pngme. While, having the opportunity to consult organizations like Walmart and Google.


Currently.

-Leading a Machine Learning practice at SF startup Pngme which is a lending platform that captures and analyzes millions of data points from smartphones so that lenders can underwrite loans in emerging markets.

-Helping to build out an app for coronavirus cases and prevention in Africa. Basically there is not nearly enough information in Africa - including reporting. The goal is to help citizens have access to symptoms, preventions and GIS maps with real-time data. This will be live soon.

-With the support of Kindred.ai, I'm working on developing a robotic arm to improve the grasp and grip of the hand using CNNs. The goal is to deploy this on a robotic prosthetic arm.

-Diving deep into the energy storage market to better understand current methods and coming up with my own solution to tackle energy storage with renewables.
